About

Fervently learning; not just code but how to: document, record, share. Started learning to code on freeCodeCamp in April 2016. I am working towards the Front-End Development Certificate and hope to gain work experience with that, be it freelance or otherwise. Ideally, I would like to work in Swift and Ruby.

I plan to be a full-stack software engineer through FCC and to help non-profit organizations with their coding needs, while building my portfolio and experience. By Summer 2017, I should have completed an Associate's Degree in Computer Science for mobile development at Gateway Community College.

Taking advice from podcasts, two in particular: Code Newbie and Developer Tea, I am documenting my learning and sharing it with others. I've made a YouTube channel, a Twitter account, and started writing on Medium.

Thanks for visiting!
